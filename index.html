About Us

Vision

To totally reinvent on-campus dining by offering a quick and effective food delivery service that makes college life more enjoyable.

Mission

Our goal is to provide enticing and wholesome meals from the campus canteen to teachers, staff, and students at their doorsteps, all the while encouraging sustainability and community involvement.

Features

Seamless Food Delivery

Get your favorite Lagoon meals delivered promptly to any spot within PUP campus. ByteBite ensures your food arrives fresh and hot, wherever you are.

Social Sharing and Feeds

Stay connected with friends and discover new favorites! Share your food experiences, post reviews, and explore trending dishes with this interactive feed.

Real-Time Comments and Reviews

Engage with the PUP community by leaving comments and ratings on your orders. ByteBite helps you make informed choices with honest reviews from fellow students.

Personalized Recommendations

Learns your preferences and suggests meals you'll love. Enjoy dining experience with personalized recommendations based on your tastes and trends.
